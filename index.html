The Seddon Deadly Sins

Pride

Pride is considered the original and most serious of the seven sins. It involves an excessive belief in one's own abilities, often leading to downfall.

Envy

Envy is the sin of wanting what others have, be it status, wealth, or possessions. It can lead to bitterness and resentment.

Wrath

Wrath is intense anger and hatred towards others, often leading to violence and vengeance.

Sloth

Sloth is the avoidance of physical or spiritual work. It can manifest as laziness or a lack of effort in one's duties.

Greed

Greed involves an excessive desire for material wealth or gain, beyond what is necessary or appropriate.

Gluttony

Gluttony is overindulgence and overconsumption of food, drink, or wealth items to the point of waste.

Lust

Lust is an intense desire for sexual pleasure or gratification. It can lead to objectification of others and a disregard for moral boundaries.
